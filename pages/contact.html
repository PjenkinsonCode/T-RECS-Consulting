Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start working with our organization?
We typically begin initial assessments within 1-2 weeks of our consultation call. Implementation timelines vary based on project scope, but most organizations see meaningful improvements within 30-90 days.
Do you work with organizations of all sizes?
Yes! We work with everything from small community centers to large municipal recreation departments. Our solutions are scalable and tailored to your specific needs and budget.
What if we're already using Microsoft 365?
Perfect! Many organizations are only using 10-20% of Microsoft 365's capabilities. We'll assess your current setup and unlock features that can dramatically improve your operations and staff productivity.
Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?
Absolutely. We offer flexible support packages including user training, system monitoring, and regular optimization reviews to ensure your technology continues to serve your organization effectively.
Can you help us integrate with our existing recreation management software?
Yes! We have extensive experience with platforms like PerfectMind, ActiveNet, RecTrac, and others. We can optimize your current system and create seamless integrations with Microsoft 365 tools.
