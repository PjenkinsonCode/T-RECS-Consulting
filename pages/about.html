Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Paiden Jenkinson, Driftwood Consulting is a new and innovative business advisory firm. Our journey begins with a clear mission: to help businesses navigate change and achieve sustainable growth.

As a fresh voice in the consulting industry, we bring a unique perspective and modern approach to business strategy. We're committed to understanding each client's unique challenges and opportunities, developing tailored strategies that drive real, measurable results.

What sets us apart is our commitment to building long-term relationships with our clients. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, we work closely with you to develop customized strategies that align with your specific goals and vision.
